<strong>72090 Service Charges for hotels</strong>
<ul><li>A <b>Service Charge</b> feature was added to the hotel PMS. An item can have a service charge included in the unit price or added to the unit price as a new <b>Detailed Revenue Entry</b> line. The service charge is posted to a GL/Account (with or without VAT).  </li><li>This service charge is configured from the Hotel Setup page.</li></ul>
