<strong>79313 Cannot do early check out</strong>
<ul><li>In Accrual Accounting method, when attempting to perform an Early Checkout after Night Audit was run, if there are revenue entries for that day already marked as paid, an error is now shown indicating that those lines cannot be changed.</li></ul>
<strong>79125 Issue with upselling a room mid-stay</strong>
<ul><li>When changing room type or room number on an in-house reservation, only dates from today onward are now affected.</li><li>Room availability is no longer displayed for past dates.</li></ul>
