<strong>75139 Add integration event in InsertPOSLine procedure before POS Trans Line is inserted</strong>
<ul><li>An event was added to <b>InsertPOSLine</b> procedure so POS Trans. Line record can be modified before it is inserted.</li></ul>
<strong>75006 Refresh hotel reservation on pos after editing</strong>
<ul><li>HTL templates were updated to align with changes made on the BC pages, ensuring UI updates.</li></ul>
<strong>74925 Negative charge lines in DRE come as positive amounts in POS Sales Journal</strong>
<ul><li>There was an issue on inserting negative reservation extras into POS journal, it was changing them to positive amounts. The issue comes from North America Localization change in logic, so now, a temporary fix is provided by setting the negative sign on the price instead of the quantity.</li></ul>
<strong>74863 Open deposit management page from Unassign Deposit line on POS</strong>
<ul><li>A faster way to manage unassigned deposit lines directly from the POS was added.</li><li>When a deposit is recorded without being linked to a folio, users can now open the <b>Deposit Management</b> page directly from the <b>Unassigned Guest Deposit</b> (or <b>Unassigned Company Deposit</b>) line.</li></ul>
<strong>74490 Room Rate filters by Group Reservation when called from line discount % field on Invoice Management page</strong>
<ul><li>There was an  issue on Room Rate Change page when it was open from a reservation that belongs to a group. It was opening the group reservation instead of only the current reservation. This was fixed. </li></ul>
